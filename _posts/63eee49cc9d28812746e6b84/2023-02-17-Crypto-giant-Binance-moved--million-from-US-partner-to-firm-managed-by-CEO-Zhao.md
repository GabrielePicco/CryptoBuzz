Over the first three months of 2021, hundreds of millions flowed from the Binance.US account at California-based Silvergate Bank to trading firm Merit Peak, according to records reviewed by Reuters.

## Highlights

- Global cryptocurrency exchange Binance had secret access to a bank account belonging to its purportedly independent U.S.
- partner.
- More than $400 million flowed from the Binance.US account at California-based Silvergate Bank to a trading firm, Merit Peak Ltd, records show.
- The money transfers suggest that the global Binance exchange, which is not licensed to operate in the United States, controlled the finances of B.US, which says it is independent, says Reuters' information is "outdated" The exchange's public terms of use at the time said its dollar deposits were held at Silvergate and a Nevada-based custodian firm called Prime Trust LLC.
