Shark Tank star Kevin O’Leary, aka Mr. Wonderful, says he is not selling any of his cryptocurrencies despite the crypto market downturn.

## Highlights

- Kevin O'Leary says he is not selling any of his cryptocurrencies despite the crypto market downturn.
- O’Leary currently holds 32 positions in the digital asset space.
- He said: 'Long term, you just have to stomach it.
- You have to understand you’ll get volatility,” he stressed.
- The Shark Tank star said the collapse of a crypto token does not have a big impact on global financial markets.
- The demise of UST “educated everybody that this isn’t the way to build a stablecoin,’ he said, emphasizing that “It’s important for the education and the maturation of the market”
