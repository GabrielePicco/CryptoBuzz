The price of one Ethereum (ETH) competitor is surging as the global soccer organization FIFA launches digital art on its blockchain.

## Highlights

- Algorand (ALGO) is the official blockchain platform of FIFA.
- New users are flocking to the platform by the thousands daily with the launch of official FIFA World Cup non-fungible tokens (NFTs) New addresses making their first ALGO transaction have climbed from 5,000 a day to 20,0000 a day within three months.
- FIFA inked a sponsorship agreement with the platform in May to make the ETH rival the official platform of the global soccer organization FIFA.
- The smart contract protocol jumped from a seven-day low of $0.293 to a high of $1.407, marking a nearly 40% increase in just one week.
