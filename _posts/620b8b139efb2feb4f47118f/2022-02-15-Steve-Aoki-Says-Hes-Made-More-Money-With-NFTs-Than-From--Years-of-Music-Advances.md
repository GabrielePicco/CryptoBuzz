NFTs could prove essential for artists, said Steve Aoki, adding that \"labels will have to do more than just add the song on a playlist.\"

## Highlights

- Steve Aoki gave the opening talk at a Gala Music event at the Forum in Inglewood, California, on February 10.
- NFTs are unique tokens that exist on a blockchain like Ether or Solana.
- Aoki wants to see these unique assets transform the music industry, he said.
- He also owns a number of Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC) NFTS, he’s worked on a Solana-based NFT marketplace and is launching Aokiverse—a membership club based on NFT tokens that will “coexist with the real world” Aoki said that his live DJ gigs probably make up 95% of his music income.
