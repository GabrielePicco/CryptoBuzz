## Highlights

- According to blockchain data provider Nansen, approximately $831 million was withdrawn from Binance in the past 24 hours.
- Users withdrew about $2.8 billion in digital assets, surpassing the $2 billion in deposits within the same timeframe.
- This marks the largest outflow from the exchange since November 2021, when FTX went bankrupt, and during the FUD surrounding the exchange in December 2022.
- Binance CEO Changpeng Zhao claims the exchange “does not owe anyone’s money,” and that everything will soon return to its previous trajectory.
- However, in an AMA afterwards, Zhao remained calm and said it was a normal market reaction, and Binance would continue to grow.
