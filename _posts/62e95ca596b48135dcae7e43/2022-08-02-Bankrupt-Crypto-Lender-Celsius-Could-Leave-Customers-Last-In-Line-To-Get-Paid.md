## Highlights

- Celsius Network filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in federal court the Southern District of New York.
- Celsius' Chief Executive Officer Alex Mashinsky disclosed a roughly $1.2 billion hole in the company’s balance sheet.
- Celsius said it owes consumer users (as opposed to institutional partners) more than $4.7 billion.
- Celsius held itself out as a safe alternative to traditional banks and promised users high interest rates.
- Many Celsius customers have written to the Bankruptcy Court, arguing to get access to their funds.
- This begs the question of when and if Celsius’ customers will be able to recoup some or any of their losses.
