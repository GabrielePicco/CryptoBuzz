Coinbase CEO Brian Armstrong is forecasting a timeline for global crypto adoption, saying that the industry is still in its very early days.

## Highlights

- Brian Armstrong is forecasting a timeline for global crypto adoption, saying that the industry is still in its early days.
- Armstrong says Bitcoin’s market capitalization is not yet big enough for BTC to act as a serious flight-to-safety asset that some of its advocates have been predicting.
- He says Bitcoin will manage to fulfil the role of a “new gold” asset class within the next decade.
- Armstrong: “I could see in the next five or ten years as the crypto economy really becomes a bigger percentage of the global GDP that people will actually flee to Bitcoin as the sort of ‘new gold' if you will’
