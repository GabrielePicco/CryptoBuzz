Ethereum Cofounder, Vitalik Buterian affirms that the ETH merge to happen on September 15th 2022.

## Highlights

- Vitalik Buterin affirms that the ETH merge to happen on September 15th, 2022.
- Terminal total difficulty has been set to 58750000000000000000000.
- This means the ethereum PoW network now has a (roughly) fixed number of hashes left to mine.
- Vitalik stated that bordel.wtf predicts the merge will take place by Sept 15, though the exact date depends on the hashrate.
- As the terminal difficulty is now 5875 difficulty has been set, this means the network has no more than 60,000 hashcodes left to work on.
