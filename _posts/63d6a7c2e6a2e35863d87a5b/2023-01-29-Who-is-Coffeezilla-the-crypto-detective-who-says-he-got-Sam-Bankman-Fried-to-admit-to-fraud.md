Meet Coffeezilla, the engineering student-turned-YouTuber who says he got Sam Bankman-Fried to admit to committing fraud as CEO of FTX.

## Highlights

- Stephen Findeisen, aka Coffeezilla, is known as a "internet detective" on YouTube.
- He interviewed Sam Bankman-Fried, the CEO of FTX, before he was arrested and charged with fraud.
- He says he got the "smoking gun" when he got FTX CEO to admit to fraud.
- Illicit crypto transactions notched an all-time high in 2022, the same year he gained over a million new subscribers on YouTube, according to a report published last year.
- Findeis says he began investigating dubious crypto projects because the industry is swarmed by fraudsters and scam artists.
- Recently, he feuded with YouTuber Logan Paul over a crypto game he said was a scam.
